❓ Frequently Asked Questions

1. How do I book a vehicle?

Just click on your preferred vehicle, choose date/time, and complete the booking. Then go to My Bookings section and make the payment.

2. What payment methods are accepted?

We accept all major UPI, debit/credit cards via Razorpay.

3. Can I cancel my booking?

You can cancel a booking from My Bookings if unpaid. Paid bookings are not cancellable.

4. Where is the pickup location?

All vehicles are picked up from our garage: RentARide Garage, Bypass Road, Indore.
